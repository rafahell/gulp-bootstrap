  • With the extraction and combustion of coal increasingly viewed negatively by consumers and regulators due to health and environmental risks, the client required a robust stewardship and brand placement strategy
  • Social media is where the opponents and detractors are most active

The Solution

  • Accenture took an innovative approach by using sentiment analysis technology for social listening and risk quantification
  • The Accenture risk management team customized and implemented a social listening technology to mine data for stakeholder conversations regarding coal across social media channels and provide the results in an easily comprehensible format

Solution Design

  • Accenture Social Media Intelligence Framework was adopted to create the solution
  • SMI framework provided valuable input for designing the social media analytics solution and developing a new coal stewardship strategy

Business Benefits

    Top line impact

  • Lower probability of revenue risks
  • Improved brand perception and value as well as company image

    Bottom line impact

  • Using detailed analytics on the external regulatory and reputational environment allow the client to optimize spending and increase the effectiveness of various outreach initiatives
  • Constant social media analysis enables the client to continually refine and adapt its business strategy and mitigate potential risks
