The Accenture Insights Platform can be customized to individual client needs across functions and industries. The business benefits vary by the clients situation and can be summarized into five broad business results:

Digital Consumer Analytics:

Accenture’s Consumer Digital Journey captures customer interaction across channels to form an integrated view for maximum influence on engagement using data-driven holistic view of the customer experience.

Social Network Analytics:

Social Network Analytics studies connected users and propagation of events to build viral models that estimate the influence. Measures the behaviour influence to identify the products / services and communities where exist more capacity of influence in customers.

Data Monetization:

Data monetization helps in generating new revenue streams from data held inside organisations, by processing the data into usable forms, or generating usable observations and insights from it.

Churn Modeling:

Churn management helps in identify customers who more likely to churn, identify root causes, enhances the customer lifecycle value, and drive positive customer experience. Predictive statistical models are used to create customer churn propensity score including logistic regression, decision tree etc.

Segmentation:

Segmentation divides the customer base into heterogeneous groups using attributes like purchasing behavior, demographics etc. for enhanced customer management.
