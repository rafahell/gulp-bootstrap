Pricing Intelligence & Insight App

Pricing Intelligence & Insight provides pricing recommendations by analyzing pricing and sales trends of clients and competitors products and calculating pricing elasticity.

The app leverages indexing, price response curve and regression techniques to conduct pricing sensitivity analysis to predict sales volumes.

The app has the potential to improve gross margins by 2-5%.

Intelligent Collections App

Intelligent Collections allows organizations to provide actionable recommendations for collection strategy by segmenting customers on multiple variables and predicting disputed invoices. The app provides descriptive analytics on accounts receivables, uses K means clustering to profile and segment customers, uses binary logistic regression to predict customers with high probability of paying late and uses predictive analytics to predict disputes. The app has the potential to effect an increase in cash collection by 10 - 25%.

Spend Optimizer App

Spend Optimizer analyses historical procurement spends data to identify strategic sourcing opportunities, recommend ways to rationalize the supplier base, enable value tracking, higher compliance monitoring, recommend procurement lead time optimization and decrease the proportion of non-contract spend. The app uses advanced analytical techniques, such as spend profiling, internal benchmarking, working capital simulator, share of business rationalization and vendor normalization & categorization, to generate actionable analytical insights and recommendations to optimize spend under management. The app has the potential to identify up to 50% vendor base rationalization opportunities; improve spend compliance up to 30% and reduce early payments leading to potential working capital benefit of 5-10%.

Campaign Manager App

The Campaign Manager App enables organizations to track the performance of it’s digital campaigns across multiple touchpoints including Paid Search, Display and Social Media. By using data blending techniques, it provides a snapshot of both the upper funnel metrics (Website visits, engagements) as well as conversions to Product Leads, and finally Orders, Sales. By quickly merging Web Traffic Data as well as Leads Data, the App provides a timely update on the performance of each campaign, allowing marketing managers to quickly identify pain points in the funnel and take steps to address them.
